About

I’m an Azeri-Iranian woman based in Dublin, Ireland. I work as a Language Lead & RTL UX Specialist. I have also worked as an iOS developer, producing several applications for various companies, in addition to RadioGeek, which was a personal project aimed at people behind the walls of filternet who want to access information freely. Along with my full-time jobs, I’ve done a lot of freelance linguistics jobs. I used to work as a technical support at an ISP, and teach English to adults as a foreign language back in Iran. Nowadays, I teach Persian as a foreign language in the evenings.
